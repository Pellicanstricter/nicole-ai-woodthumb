Make Something Real With Your Hands

Wood Thumb is a traditional San Francisco woodshop offering hands-on woodworking classes where you learn new skills and leave with a finished project. Located in the SOMA district, we provide a welcoming space for beginners and experienced woodworkers alike.

Whether you're looking to take a workshop, rent shop time, or plan a team building event, we're here to help you create something beautiful.
